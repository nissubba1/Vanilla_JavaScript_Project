FAQ

What is the purpose of Atlas Informatics?

+

Atlas Informatics is way to view each country from high level point of view.

Does Atlas Informatics have each country in the world?

+

Yes, Atlas Informatics contains high level information about all countries on the globe. The information presented here is directly from RESTCountries API.

Does Atlas Informatics updated their information?

+

Yes, since Atlas Informatics is using REST API, whenever it gets updated, the information presented here will also be automatically updated.
